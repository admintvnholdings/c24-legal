Privacy Policy

Connect24 cc — A TVN Holdings Trust company

Last updated: 8 March 2026

Connect24 cc is an installer, service, and integration company operating as part of the TVN Holdings Trust group of companies, based in Windhoek, Namibia. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information through our applications and services.

1. Information We Collect

We process the following categories of data in the course of business operations:

  • Business contact information: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and company details of customers, suppliers, and business partners as stored in our CRM and business systems.
  • Employee information: Names, roles, and work contact details of staff members for internal workflow automation.
  • Service data: Site addresses, equipment details, service history, and project information related to installations and maintenance.
  • Communication metadata: Message timestamps, sender/recipient identifiers, and channel information for business communications routed through integrated platforms (including WhatsApp and Meta services).

We do not collect personal data from the general public beyond what is necessary for service delivery.

2. How We Use Information

Data processed by Connect24 is used solely for:

  • Service delivery, installation scheduling, and maintenance operations
  • Customer relationship management and sales
  • Business communications and customer support
  • Quotation, invoicing, and billing
  • Reporting and analytics for business operations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Data may be shared:

  • Between TVN Holdings Trust group entities (Connect24, StratoVerse, EdgeX360) for legitimate business purposes such as procurement, project delivery, and billing
  • With third-party service providers (Zoho, Meta, Microsoft) that process data on our behalf under appropriate data processing agreements
  • When required by law or regulation applicable in Namibia

4. Data Storage and Security

Data is stored on secured cloud platforms with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to authorised personnel on a need-to-know basis.

5. Data Retention

Business data is retained for the duration required by Namibian law and legitimate business need. When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable Namibian data protection law (including the Namibia Data Protection Bill and constitutional privacy rights), you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data held by us
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (see our Data Deletion Instructions)
  • Object to processing of your data

7. Third-Party Services

Our applications may integrate with third-party platforms including Meta (Facebook, WhatsApp, Instagram). When you interact with us through these platforms, their respective privacy policies also apply.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
