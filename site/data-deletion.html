Data Deletion Instructions

Connect24 cc — A TVN Holdings Trust company

Last updated: 8 March 2026

If you wish to have your personal data deleted from Connect24's systems, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Send an email to admin@c-24.net with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

Please include in your request:

  1. Your full name
  2. The email address(es) associated with your data
  3. The company or organisation you are associated with (if applicable)
  4. A description of the data you would like deleted

What Happens Next

  1. We will acknowledge your request within 5 business days.
  2. We will verify your identity to ensure the request is legitimate.
  3. Once verified, your data will be deleted from all active systems within 30 days.
  4. You will receive confirmation once the deletion is complete.

What Data Is Deleted

Upon a valid deletion request, we will remove:

  • Your contact information from our CRM and business systems
  • Any communication history associated with your account
  • Any service records linked to your identity
  • Any other personal data linked to your identity in our systems

Exceptions

Certain data may be retained where required by Namibian law (e.g., financial transaction records for tax compliance, or records required for ongoing contractual obligations). In such cases, we will inform you of what is retained and why.
